About Us

The Shelter‑Stead provides a safe, engaging and enriching home‑away‑from‑home where pets thrive through positive reinforcement (R+) training and compassionate care. We’re committed to small staff‑to‑pet ratios, daily progress reports and science‑based methods that respect each pet’s individuality.

Our Services

Dog Boarding

Overnight stays in comfy suites with daily playtime, enrichment and cuddle sessions. Includes photo updates to keep you connected.

Cat Boarding

Quiet, private condos with enrichment toys and climbing perches. We provide the purrfect mix of play and relaxation.

Daycare & Play

Structured daycare programs separated by size and energy level. Includes indoor/outdoor play, puzzle time and rest breaks.

Training Programs

Group classes, private lessons and board‑and‑train programs using R+ techniques. Designed to enhance the bond between you and your pet.
